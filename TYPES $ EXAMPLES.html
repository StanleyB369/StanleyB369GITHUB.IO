TYPES OF E-WASTE

·         Computer and computer periptierals

·         Tvs and other home electronic

·         Small house hold appliances

·         Solar panels

·         Medical device

Electronic waste (e-waste) is any device that has reached the end of its working life and contains electrical components, power, or battery supply. E-waste can include:
  • Electronics: Computers, mobile phones, tablets, monitors, and printers
  • Home appliances: Air conditioners, refrigerators, freezers, and other cooling equipment
  • Lighting: LED lamps, fluorescent lamps, high intensity discharge lamps, and sodium vapor lamps
  • Other: VCRs, portable DVD players, vending machines, and solar panels
